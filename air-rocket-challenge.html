Air Rocket Design Challenge

Design and build an air rocket that launches safely, flies consistently, and demonstrates strong assembly design through fit, fin design, testing, and iteration.

Challenge Overview

You will design and build a rocket that launches using compressed air. Your goal is to create a stable, repeatable flight by applying proper engineering design principles.

Design Constraints
  • Modify provided rocket body design
  • Create one unique fin design
  • Fins must use a press-fit
  • Body must use a slip-fit on launcher
  • Must launch safely and consistently
Engineering Goals
  • Improve stability and flight consistency
  • Design effective fin geometry
  • Apply proper fit tolerances
  • Use testing and iteration to improve performance
Project Timeline (5 Days)
Day 1: Intro, sketching, design planning
Day 2: CAD + fin design
Day 3: Build + fabrication
Day 4: Testing and iteration
Day 5: Final launch + evaluation
