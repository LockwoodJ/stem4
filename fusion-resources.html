Fusion Tutorial Completion Checklist

Students should complete the following items as they work through the tutorial library.

Watch each tutorial video and follow along in Autodesk Fusion.
Save each Fusion file using a clear file name.
Take screenshots showing progress or completed models.
Identify at least three Fusion tools used during the tutorials.
Explain one mistake or challenge and how it was fixed.
Submit evidence of work in the assigned location.

Student Assignment

Complete the Fusion tutorial sequence and submit a short evidence document or engineering notebook entry.

  1. Complete all four tutorial videos.
  2. Include at least four screenshots, one from each tutorial.
  3. List at least three Fusion tools you used.
  4. Explain which tool was easiest to use and which tool was most challenging.
  5. Choose one tutorial model and make a small design modification of your own.
  6. Submit your screenshots, reflection, and modified design evidence.
