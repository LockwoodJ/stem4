Support the FabLab

The FabLab helps students design, prototype, test, and build real engineering projects. Donations help keep materials, tools, and consumables available for student work.

Every Supply Helps

Small consumables often make the biggest difference in daily project work.

Donation Wishlist

These items directly support student design, prototyping, fabrication, and classroom engineering challenges.

3D Printing Supplies

  • PLA filament
  • Build plate cleaning supplies
  • Storage bags or bins
  • Replacement nozzles
  • Glue sticks or bed adhesion supplies

Laser / Craft Materials

  • Cardboard sheets
  • Basswood or plywood sheets
  • Acrylic sheets if approved
  • Masking tape or transfer tape
  • Rulers and measuring tools

Classroom Consumables

  • Hot glue sticks
  • Painter's tape
  • Sandpaper
  • Zip ties
  • Craft sticks and dowels

Hand Tool Support

  • Safety glasses
  • Small clamps
  • Precision screwdrivers
  • Needle-nose pliers
  • Cutting mats

Organization

  • Clear storage bins
  • Label tape
  • Parts organizers
  • Drawer dividers
  • Clipboards

Project Materials

  • Foam board
  • Poster board
  • Rubber bands
  • String or fishing line
  • Small hardware packs

What Donations Support

Donated supplies help students move from idea to prototype faster.

Student Learning

  • More opportunities to prototype and test
  • More design iteration instead of one-and-done builds
  • More access to fabrication materials
  • More realistic engineering problem solving

Before Donating

  • Please avoid unknown chemicals or unsafe materials.
  • Ask before donating electronics, tools, or large items.
  • Materials should be clean and classroom appropriate.
  • Consumables and organization supplies are always useful.
