Safety First

Certification means you are prepared to work safely, responsibly, and with instructor approval.

Certification Steps

Follow these steps before using machines, submitting fabrication files, or working independently in the FabLab.

01

General FabLab Safety

Review workspace expectations, PPE, tool boundaries, cleanup procedures, and how to report problems.

02

Tool-Specific Training

Complete the required training for the equipment you want to use, such as 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC, or hand tools.

03

Instructor Approval

Do not operate equipment until your instructor has approved your access level and confirmed that your file or task is safe.

Tool Access Expectations

Different tools require different levels of supervision and preparation.

Usually Requires File Review

  • 3D printing files
  • Laser cutting and engraving files
  • CAD models prepared for fabrication
  • Projects using shared materials

Usually Requires Direct Approval

  • CNC toolpaths or machine setup
  • Power tools
  • New materials or unknown materials
  • Any tool you have not been trained to use

Before You Use Any Tool

Pause and check that you are ready.

  • I know what tool I need and why I need it.
  • I understand the safety expectations for that tool.
  • My file, material, or setup has been checked.
  • I know what could go wrong and when to stop.
  • I know how to clean up and return the workspace to ready condition.
