When to Use Resources

  • Before starting a new project or unit activity.
  • When your CAD model is not behaving correctly.
  • Before submitting a 3D print or laser cut request.
  • When your engineering notebook needs stronger evidence.

Before Asking for Review

  • Check your file name and file type.
  • Confirm your notebook shows your design process.
  • Include screenshots, photos, sketches, or test evidence.
  • Make sure your work matches the project requirements.
