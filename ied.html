Unit Roadmap

These units guide students through the major skills and habits used throughout the engineering design process.

01

Design Process

Problem definition, criteria, constraints, research, brainstorming, and decision making.

02

Technical Sketching

Isometric sketching, multiview drawings, annotation, and visual communication.

03

Measurement

Precision, accuracy, units, calipers, dimensioning, and tolerance awareness.

04

CAD Modeling

Sketch constraints, 3D features, assemblies, file organization, and model accuracy.

05

Engineering Drawings

Views, dimensions, title blocks, annotations, and technical communication.

06

Product Improvement

Testing, iteration, redesign, presentation, reflection, and final documentation.

IED Expectations

Strong engineering work shows the process, not just the final answer.

Before You Build

  • Define the problem clearly.
  • Identify criteria and constraints.
  • Create sketches before modeling.
  • Plan how your design will be tested.

Before You Submit

  • Check file names and file types.
  • Include screenshots, sketches, or photos.
  • Explain what changed during revision.
  • Submit only when files are ready for review.

Ready to fabricate?

Make sure your file is complete, named clearly, and ready for review before submitting.
