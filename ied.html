What You’ll Do in This Course

  • Learn and apply the engineering design process.
  • Develop technical sketching and visualization skills.
  • Create and modify designs using CAD software such as Fusion 360.
  • Build, test, and improve physical prototypes.
  • Work through real-world design challenges with clear constraints.
  • Document your work in a professional engineering notebook.

Start Strong

  • Check the daily agenda at the beginning of class.
  • Keep your engineering notebook organized and updated.
  • Save CAD files with clear names and versions.
  • Ask for feedback before your design is finished.
  • Use constraints as design tools, not obstacles.

How to Be Successful in IED

Good engineers are organized, reflective, creative, and willing to improve their ideas.

Stay Organized Keep files, sketches, notes, and project work easy to find.
Ask Early Get help before small problems become major setbacks.
Iterate Often Your first idea is rarely your final design. Improve it.
Document Clearly Show your thinking, decisions, evidence, and revisions.
Build With Purpose Every model, sketch, and prototype should solve a problem.
